Our Story

Where Operations Meets Intelligence

Crecita was founded on a simple but powerful observation: as AI moved from research into production, the gap between building models and operating them reliably had become the most expensive problem in technology.

Our founders came from both sides - engineering infrastructure at scale and deploying ML systems in regulated industries. They saw teams reinventing the same wheel, and decided to build the practice that bridges DevOps discipline with AI-native operations.

Today, Crecita operates across 7 service domains - from traditional CI/CD to agentic AI systems - all underpinned by a certified security practice that ensures every system we build is built to last, and built to be trusted.

Our Mission

To be the operational backbone that lets enterprises deploy AI confidently - fast, secure, and at scale.

Our Vision

A world where every organisation - regardless of size - can harness AI safely and sustainably.
