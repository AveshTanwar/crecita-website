The Challenge

A Team Drowning in Alert Noise

A fast-growing B2B SaaS platform was generating 800+ alerts per day — the vast majority false positives or low-priority noise. On-call engineers couldn't distinguish real incidents from noise. Critical failures were getting buried. MTTR had crept to 4.2 hours. Team morale was at an all-time low, and key senior engineers were threatening to leave.

The monitoring stack wasn't wrong — it was generating signal. But there was no intelligence layer to correlate, group, or prioritise that signal. Every alert was treated equally, so nothing was treated urgently.

Our Solution

Intelligent Noise Suppression — Without Ripping Out Your Stack

We deployed a layered AIOps solution that sat on top of the existing monitoring infrastructure, bringing machine learning-powered correlation and root cause analysis without disrupting what was already working.

  • Deployed Moogsoft for intelligent event correlation and noise suppression across all services
  • Integrated seamlessly with existing Datadog and PagerDuty stack — zero rip-and-replace
  • Trained ML models on 12 months of historical incident data to understand normal vs. anomalous
  • Built automated root cause analysis workflows with contextual enrichment
  • Implemented smart alert grouping, deduplication, and suppression rules
  • Created AI-assisted on-call runbooks with suggested remediation steps
  • Delivered Prometheus + Grafana dashboards for real-time operational visibility
