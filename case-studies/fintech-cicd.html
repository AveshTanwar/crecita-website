The Problem

Weekly Deploys. War Rooms. Fear.

The payments platform was shipping once a week — every deployment required a war room, manual testing, and dedicated weekend work. Developer morale was low, releases were feared, and business agility was impossible.

Test coverage sat at 34%. Any failure meant hours of rollback. The team was trapped in a cycle of reactive firefighting rather than building the product.

Before Crecita
  • 1 deploy per week, manually
  • 34% test coverage
  • Hours to rollback on failure
  • Weekend war rooms required
What We Built

A Complete CI/CD Rebuild — From the Ground Up

Crecita rebuilt the entire CI/CD pipeline from scratch, replacing fragile manual processes with automated, declarative, production-grade delivery infrastructure.

  • GitHub Actions pipelines with automated test gates
  • Containerised microservices with Docker and Kubernetes (EKS)
  • GitOps workflow using ArgoCD for declarative deployments
  • Helm charts for consistent, version-controlled releases
  • Terraform for reproducible infrastructure provisioning
  • Automated test coverage: 34% → 91%

The Results

Shipping Became a Non-Event

What was once the most stressful day of the week became a background process. The team now focuses on building — not deploying.

  • Team now ships to production 12 times per day — zero manual intervention required
  • Rollback takes under 60 seconds — any incident is contained before it becomes a crisis
  • Test coverage rose from 34% to 91% — catching bugs before they ever reach production
  • Developer confidence and morale transformed — engineers are now proud to ship
  • Business can respond to market changes same-day — agility is now a competitive advantage
