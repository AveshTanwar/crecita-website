MLOps · Healthcare

6-Week Model Releases Cut to 3 Days

A healthcare analytics company had a talented data science team producing strong models - but their path to production involved manual handoffs, undocumented experiments, and no monitoring once deployed.

Crecita implemented a full MLOps platform: MLflow for experiment tracking, Kubeflow for pipeline automation, a centralised feature store, and Evidently AI for real-time drift detection with automated retraining triggers.

Result: Model deployment time dropped from 6 weeks to 3 days. Prediction accuracy improved 40% through systematic experimentation. Zero silent model failures since go-live.

AIOps · SaaS Platform

800 Daily Alerts Reduced to 35 Actionable Incidents

A B2B SaaS platform's on-call team was drowning. 800+ alerts per day, most of them noise. Engineers were burning out, real incidents were getting lost, and MTTR was measured in hours.

Crecita deployed an AIOps layer using Moogsoft for event correlation, integrated with their existing Datadog and PagerDuty stack. ML models were trained on 12 months of historical incident data to identify patterns and auto-suppress low-signal noise.

Result: Alert volume reduced by 96%. On-call engineers now see only meaningful incidents. MTTR dropped from 4.2 hours to 22 minutes.

LLMOps · Legal Tech

Enterprise LLM at Scale - 52% Cost Cut

A legal technology firm had built an internal document Q&A tool on GPT-4 - but costs were spiralling, latency was unacceptable, and the team had no visibility into hallucination rates or answer quality.

Crecita redesigned their RAG pipeline with optimised chunking and re-ranking, replaced full GPT-4 calls with a fine-tuned smaller model for routine queries, and introduced LangSmith for full observability including hallucination tracking.

Result: Token costs cut by 52%, response latency down 68%, and answer accuracy climbed to 94% through better retrieval. The team now has full visibility into every LLM interaction.

CyberSecurity · E-commerce

SOC 2 Compliance in 14 Weeks

A rapidly scaling e-commerce platform was losing enterprise deals because they couldn't produce a SOC 2 Type II report. Their security posture was ad-hoc, documentation was non-existent, and they had no formalised incident response process.

Crecita's CISA and CISM certified team led the full compliance program: gap assessment, control implementation, SIEM deployment with Splunk, evidence collection, and audit preparation. All within a 14-week timeline to meet a contract deadline.

Result: SOC 2 Type II achieved within the deadline. 3 enterprise contracts signed within 60 days. Zero critical findings in the audit.
